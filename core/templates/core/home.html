OUR STORY

Established in 1965, we are more than just a tattoo studio and barbershop we are a living legacy of creativity and craftsmanship in Ennis. Step into our world of ink, clippers, and piercings, where hair artistry and tattoo artistry come together to create an unforgettable experience. At Inked Clippers, we believe in the power of self-expression through the medium of art. Our team of talented barbers, skilled tattoo artists, and expert piercers are passionate about translating your unique style and personality into personalized masterpieces. Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut that reflects your personality, an intricate tattoo that tells your story, or a stylish piercing to accentuate your individuality, we are here to bring your vision to life.

With over 50 years of heritage, Inked Clippers has a rich history of serving the community with exceptional barbering, tattooing, and piercing services. Our journey began as Clippers, a modest barbershop founded by the visionary artist Mike O'Brien in the heart of Ennis. Over the years, we have earned the trust and loyalty of our clients, becoming an integral part of their lives and stories. Today, we proudly carry on this tradition, blending the artistry of our founder with modern techniques and innovations. In 2015, the legacy of Clippers evolved into Inked Clippers.
