History Of Inked Clippers

In 1965, the visionary artist Mike O'Brien founded "Clippers," a modest barbershop that quickly gained a reputation for excellence in Ennis. Mike's dedication to his craft and commitment to fostering meaningful connections with clients laid the groundwork for what would become our enduring legacy. As the years passed, the founder's two sons, Robert and Darren, embraced their artistic callings. Robert joined his father in the barbershop, adding his contemporary flair to the age-old craft. Together, they created a welcoming atmosphere that made clients feel like family.

Meanwhile, Darren pursued his dream of mastering tattoo artistry and piercing, embarking on a global journey to learn from the world's finest artists. His experiences abroad enriched his artistry and brought fresh perspectives to the family business. In the face of adversity and the passing of our beloved founder, Mike O'Brien, after 60 years of service, Robert assumed the reins of Clippers, ensuring the continuation of our cherished legacy. In 2015, Darren returned home, ready to merge his tattooing and piercing expertise with the established barbershop, and thus, Inked Clippers was born.

Today, our team of barbers, tattoo artists, and piercers works in harmony, transforming every visit into an immersive journey of self-discovery and self-expression. Inked Clippers stands as a testament to the power of art to unite generations and tell stories that span decades. We honor the memory of our father, Mike O'Brien, while embracing the future, welcoming clients from all walks of life into our ever-evolving family in Ennis.
